Drop hairstyle reference photos here. Perfect Corp's hair-transfer endpoint
needs an actual image of the desired hairstyle (it copies the style onto
the selfie); there is no template_id catalog for this feature.

Required filenames (referenced by apps/web/src/lib/event-templates.ts):
  loose_waves.jpg   — Date Night
  low_pony.jpg      — Job Interview
  soft_updo.jpg     — Wedding Guest
  beachy_waves.jpg  — Vacation
  messy_bun.jpg     — Casual Friday

Recommended: clear front-facing photos, neutral background, the hairstyle
clearly visible. Any face works — only the hair shape/length is transferred.
